Pay Rent - Great Rift Valley

Thank you for choosing to pay your rent for the Great Rift Valley property.

To complete your payment, please follow the instructions below:

  • Step 1: Open your M-PESA app (or any other payment platform that supports Paybill).
  • Step 2: Select 'Lipa na M-PESA' and then choose 'Paybill'.
  • Step 3: Enter the Paybill number: 247247
  • Step 4: Enter the Account Number: 748693
  • Step 5: Enter the amount you want to pay (in KES).
  • Step 6: Confirm and send the payment.

Once you have completed the payment, please fill out the form below to confirm your payment.
